Consulting And Advising

We provide consultation and assistance on emerging technologies and blockchain development. As a part of our driving mission, we believe that the free flow of information is vital to creating practicable solutions to real world problems. It is through consultation and assistance to those unfamiliar with blockchain that we enable unique products to be created that would otherwise use traditional market solutions.

Hardware Solutions

Innovative developments in hardware are key part of bringing emerging technologies into established markets. With years of collective experience within the UniBit team, we are already working in conjunction with our global partners to find solutions for remittance markets, ATM integration, and market liquidity.

Software Solutions

Unibit Labs provides public and/or private aplication development assistance on the UniBit Platform. Applications can be built in a decentralized and public manner, available to any user who seeks to access it. In addition, we can build private closed source applications for public or private entities who need secure and protected in house development.

Product Strategy

Private blockchain development is a unique opportunity that the UniBit team provides for both developers and entrepreneurs who seek to harness the power of blockchain tech. Our framework allows for both private and public blockchain creation so applications could range from a private blockchain ledger that holds private and secure data, to open source open use projects that provide new opportunities to connect emerging technologies with previously untapped users.
